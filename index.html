    One day workshop on
    Advanced Welding & Surface Engineering[AWSE]

    AWSE is the first major event being organized by IIW Hyderabad Branch. Considering the technical relevance and overlapping technologies, the workshop is designed to cover the two important themes: Welding & Surface Engineering.


    The event is designed in such a way that there will be takeaways for everybody who is involved in welding and surface engineering such as: Industry professionals, R&D scientist, academics & students. The programme includes:


    • Invited lectures by eminent experts;
    • Expert panel discussion to address some technical and practical industrial applications;
    • contributory papers in the form if poster presentation; and
    • Short industry presentations on equipment/products.
